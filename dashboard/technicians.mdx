The Technicians page shows every field tech in your roster as a card grid. Each card shows their contact details, skills, current status, and workload at a glance.

Navigate to **Dashboard → Technicians** to open it.

## Status Tabs

Filter your roster by real-time status using the tabs at the top:

| Tab | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **All** | Every technician in the system |
| **On Job** | Technicians currently dispatched to a job site |
| **Available** | Technicians with no current assignment |
| **Off Duty** | Technicians not available for dispatch |

A count summary appears at the top right: total technicians, how many are on a job, and how many are available right now.

## Date Range Picker

Click the date range button in the header to filter jobs by a specific time window. The page shows each technician's job count within the selected period — useful for checking workload distribution before planning a new day.

## Searching

Use the search bar to find technicians by **name** or **skill**. The list filters in real time as you type.

## Technician Cards

Each card shows:

| Field | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Avatar** | Color-coded circle with technician's initials |
| **Name** | Full name |
| **Status** | Live status (On Job, Available, or Off Duty) with a colored dot |
| **Current job** | Name of the job they're currently at, or "No active job" |
| **Skills** | Skill badges for each certified skill |
| **Phone** | Mobile number on file |
| **Jobs & availability** | Job count in the selected period + number of days available this week |

Click any card to open the technician's full profile page.

## Where Technician Data Comes From

Technicians are synced from your connected scheduling software (Jobber, FieldCamp, HouseCall Pro, or ServiceTrade). You can't add technicians manually here — they need to be created in your FSM first, then they'll appear here after the next sync.

<Note>
If a technician is missing from this list, make sure they exist in your scheduling software and that your integration is connected under **Settings → Connected Apps**. Then wait for the next sync or refresh the page.
</Note>

## How Skills Affect Dispatch

The AI Dispatcher uses the skills listed here when deciding which technician gets which job. If a job requires a specific certification and a technician doesn't have it listed, the AI won't assign them — even if they're nearby and available.

<Tip>
Keep skills up to date in your scheduling software. Missing or outdated skills are one of the most common reasons the AI skips a capable technician.
</Tip>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A technician shows as Off Duty — what does that mean?">
    They've been marked as unavailable in your scheduling software and won't receive new assignments. If you need them back on the roster, update their status in your FSM.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why doesn't this technician show up in AI Dispatch suggestions?">
    Check that they're marked as **Available** (not Off Duty) and have at least one skill listed. Off-duty technicians and those with no skills are excluded from optimization.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit a technician's skills from this page?">
    Click the card to open their profile and update details there. However, skill changes also need to be made in your scheduling software so they stay in sync. Always update both places to avoid discrepancies.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
