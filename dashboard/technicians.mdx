The Technicians page shows every field tech in your roster — their contact details, skills, location, and status. This is where you check who's active, who's on hold, and who still needs to accept their invitation.

Navigate to **Dashboard → Technicians** to open it.

## What You'll See

Each row in the table is one technician. Here's what each column means:

| Column | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Technician's full name with a color-coded avatar |
| **Email** | Their email address (click to open your mail app) |
| **Phone** | Mobile number on file |
| **Skills** | Number of skills or certifications in the system |
| **Location** | City and state from their home address |
| **Status** | Whether they're Active, Inactive, or Pending |
| **Jobs** | Total jobs assigned to this technician |
| **Created** | When they were added to the system |

## Status Types

| Status | What It Means |
|---|---|
| 🟢 **Active** | Available for job assignments |
| ⚫ **Inactive** | Not currently available — won't receive new assignments |
| 🟡 **Pending** | Invitation sent but not yet accepted |

## Filtering Your Roster

Use the tabs at the top to filter by status:

- **All technicians** — Everyone in the system
- **Active** — Only technicians currently available for dispatch
- **Inactive** — Technicians on hold or no longer with the team
- **Pending invitation** — Technicians who haven't accepted their invitation yet

## Technician Actions

Click the **⋯ button** at the end of any row to access actions for that technician:

- **View profile** — Open their full profile with all details
- **Edit technician** — Update their name, contact info, or skills
- **View jobs** — See all jobs assigned to them
- **Deactivate** — Mark them as inactive (they'll stop receiving new assignments)

## Column Filters and Toolbar

Use the filter chips above the table to narrow by **Status**, **Skills**, **Location**, or **Jobs**. Click **More filters** for additional options.

The right-hand toolbar provides three additional actions:

| Button | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Export** | Download the current filtered roster as a report |
| **Analyse** | Run a workload analysis across the visible technicians |
| **Edit columns** | Show or hide individual columns in the table |

## Where Technician Data Comes From

Technicians are synced from your connected scheduling software (Jobber, FieldCamp, HousecallPro, or ServiceTrade). You can't add technicians manually here — they need to be created in your FSM first, then they'll appear here after the next sync.

<Note>
If a technician is missing from this list, make sure they exist in your scheduling software and that your integration is connected under **Settings → Connected Apps**. Then wait for the next sync or click the refresh button.
</Note>

## How Skills Affect Dispatch

The AI Dispatcher uses the skills listed here when deciding which technician gets which job. If a job requires plumbing certification and a technician doesn't have it listed, the AI won't assign them to that job — even if they're nearby and available.

<Tip>
Keep skills up to date in your scheduling software. Outdated or missing skills are one of the most common reasons the AI skips a technician for a job they could actually do.
</Tip>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit a technician's skills from this page?">
    The **Edit technician** action lets you update details. However, skill changes also need to be made in your scheduling software so they stay in sync. Always update both places to avoid discrepancies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A technician shows as Pending — what should I do?">
    They were invited but haven't accepted yet. You can resend the invitation from their profile, or check with them directly. Pending technicians don't appear in dispatch suggestions until they activate their account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why doesn't this technician show up in AI Dispatch?">
    Check that they're marked as **Active** and have at least one skill listed. Inactive technicians and those with no skills are excluded from optimization.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
