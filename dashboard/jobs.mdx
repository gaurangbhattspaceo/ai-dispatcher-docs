# Jobs

The Jobs page is your dispatch queue — it shows all jobs imported from your FSM platform, their readiness for dispatch, and the results of AI optimization.

## Queue Tabs

Jobs are organized into three tabs:

| Tab | What It Shows |
|-----|--------------|
| **Queue** | Jobs synced from your FSM that are ready (or pending readiness) for dispatch |
| **Dispatched** | Jobs sent to the AI optimizer — shows assigned technician and timing |
| **Confirmed** | Jobs with accepted AI suggestions that have been written back to your FSM |

## Syncing Jobs

Click **Sync from [FSM name]** to pull the latest jobs and technicians from your connected platform. The sync also checks each job's dispatch readiness (skills matched, time window valid, location geocoded).

## Batch Dispatch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select jobs">
    On the **Queue** tab, check the boxes next to the jobs you want to dispatch. You can select any combination of ready and not-yet-ready jobs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send to optimizer">
    Click the **Dispatch Selected** button that appears above the list. If no individual jobs are selected, the button changes to **Dispatch All Ready** — which sends all ready jobs in one batch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review suggestions">
    Switch to the **AI Dispatch** page to review the optimization results and accept or reject each assignment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Jobs flagged as **Ready** have passed all pre-dispatch checks. Jobs marked **Not Ready** are missing required data (e.g., no matching skill or unresolved address). Fix the underlying issue in your FSM and re-sync to make them ready.
</Tip>

## Dispatched Tab

Once jobs are sent to the optimizer, they appear in the **Dispatched** tab. Each row shows:

- Job title and customer details
- The technician the AI assigned
- Estimated arrival and departure time
- Dispatch timestamp

## Job Details

Each job row includes:
- Customer name and service address
- Required skills and job type
- Time window (scheduled date/time)
- Current status badge
- Sync timestamp

## Job Status Badges

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Ready** | All required data is present; job can be dispatched |
| **Not Ready** | Missing data — address, skills, or time window needs attention |
| **Dispatched** | Sent to the AI optimizer |
| **Confirmed** | AI suggestion accepted and written back to FSM |
