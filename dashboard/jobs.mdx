# Jobs

The Jobs page displays all service jobs in your system — imported from your FSM platform or created manually.

## Job List

View all jobs with filtering and sorting by status, priority, technician, service area, and date.

## Creating Jobs

Jobs can be added in three ways:
1. **Automatic sync** from your connected FSM platform
2. **Manual creation** via the "New Job" button
3. **CSV import** for bulk job creation

## Job Details

Each job includes:
- Customer information and address
- Required skills and equipment
- Time window and priority
- Assigned technician (if dispatched)
- Job status and history
