# Capacity Planning

The Capacity Planning page gives you a real-time snapshot of how your team's workload is distributed. Spot overloaded technicians, find available bandwidth, and jump straight to the AI Dispatch engine — all from one view.

Navigate to **Dashboard → Capacity** to open this page.

## Summary Metrics

At the top of the page, four headline numbers give you a quick health check:

| Metric | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Total jobs** | All jobs currently in your system |
| **Unplanned jobs** | Jobs not yet assigned to any technician |
| **Active technicians** | Technicians with an Active status |
| **Utilization rate** | Percentage of total capacity that is filled (assigned jobs ÷ total capacity) |

Each metric also shows a trend indicator (e.g. +12%) compared to the previous period.

<Note>
Capacity is calculated as **5 jobs per active technician** by default. If your team uses different capacity targets, contact your administrator to adjust this.
</Note>

## Filtering Technicians

Use the filter tabs beneath the metrics to focus on the technicians you care about most:

| Tab | Shows |
|---|---|
| **All technicians** | Every technician in your system |
| **Overloaded (>80%)** | Technicians whose utilization is above 80% |
| **Available (<50%)** | Technicians with significant open capacity |
| **Inactive** | Technicians with an Inactive status |

## Technician Table

The main table shows one row per technician with these columns:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Technician** | Name with color-coded avatar |
| **Skills** | Number of skills on record (e.g. "3 skills") |
| **Assigned jobs** | Jobs currently assigned to this technician |
| **Capacity** | Maximum jobs this technician can handle |
| **Utilization** | Visual bar and percentage badge — color-coded green/yellow/red |
| **Location** | City and state from the technician's address |
| **Status** | Active or Inactive |

### Utilization Color Guide

| Color | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 Green | Under 60% | Technician has plenty of available capacity |
| 🟡 Yellow | 60–80% | Healthy workload — normal operating range |
| 🔴 Red | Over 80% | Overloaded — consider reassigning jobs |

## Per-Technician Actions

Click the **⋯ menu** at the end of any row to access quick actions for that technician:

- **View details** — Open the full technician profile
- **Assign jobs** — Manually assign pending jobs to this technician
- **View schedule** — See their day-by-day schedule
- **Adjust capacity** — Change the capacity ceiling for this technician

## Toolbar Actions

The toolbar above the table provides additional filters and bulk actions:

**Filter buttons:** Utilization · Skills · Location · Jobs · More filters

**Action buttons:**
- **Export** — Download the current view as a report
- **Analyse** — Run a workload analysis across the team
- **Optimize** — Launch AI Dispatch to automatically rebalance the schedule

<Tip>
Click **Optimize** when you see several overloaded technicians alongside others with low utilization. The AI Dispatch engine will rebalance assignments to even out the workload.
</Tip>
