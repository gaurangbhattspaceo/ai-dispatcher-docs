The Calendar is your day-to-day scheduling hub. See every technician's schedule at a glance, review AI-generated job suggestions, and accept or reject assignments — all without leaving this page.

Navigate to **Dashboard → Calendar** to open it.

## View Modes

The calendar offers four views — switch between them using the **view dropdown** in the top-left header.

| View | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Calendar** | Standard weekly grid — one column per technician, one row per time slot |
| **Resource** | Per-technician focus view — choose **Day** (single day, all techs) or **Week** (one tech at a time) |
| **Timeline** | Horizontal Gantt-style view of all technicians across a day. Two sub-modes: **Schedule** (all-day overview) and **Hourly** (hour-by-hour blocks) |
| **Map** | Geographic view of technician locations and job sites — see who is closest to each job |

<Tip>
The **Resource** view remembers your last-used sub-mode (Day or Week) between sessions so you don't have to re-select it every time.
</Tip>

### Navigation

- **Calendar / Resource Week / Timeline / Map Week:** Use the **‹** and **›** arrow buttons to move backward or forward one week. Click **Today** to jump back to the current week.
- **Resource Day / Map Day:** Arrows navigate one day at a time. Click **Today** to return to today.
- Click the **calendar icon** to jump to a specific date.

## Grid Controls

Three additional dropdowns appear in the header toolbar to customize how the grid looks:

### Time Slot Granularity ⏱

Controls how many minutes each row represents. Options: **15 min**, **30 min**, **1 hour** (default), **2 hours**, **3 hours**.

- Smaller slots give a more detailed view of job timing — useful for busy days with back-to-back appointments.
- Larger slots give a high-level overview and reduce scrolling on lighter days.

### Full View / Business Hours

- **Full View** (default) — Shows the entire 24-hour day.
- **Business Hours** — Clips the grid to only show your configured working hours (set in **Settings → Company Business Hours**). Useful for keeping the calendar focused on your actual working day.

## Job Blocks

Each colored block on the calendar represents a job or visit:

| Block Color | What It Means |
|---|---|
| 🔵 Blue | AI suggestion — awaiting your approval |
| 🟢 Green | Accepted / confirmed AI assignment |
| 🟡 Yellow | Scheduled visit from your FSM |
| 🌿 Teal | In-progress visit |
| 🩷 Pink | Completed visit |
| 🟠 Orange | In-transit to a job |

Travel time blocks appear as a lighter shaded strip before each job block — this is the AI's estimated drive time built into the schedule.

## Reviewing AI Suggestions

When the AI Dispatch engine runs, its suggested assignments appear as blue blocks on the calendar.

**To accept a suggestion:**
Click the blue job block to open the detail card, then click **Accept**. The block turns green and the assignment is written back to your FSM.

**To reject a suggestion:**
Click the block and click **Reject**. You'll be asked for a brief reason — this feedback helps the AI improve over time.

**Bulk accept/reject for one technician:**
In the right panel **AI** tab, click **Accept All** or **Reject All** next to a technician's name.

<Tip>
Use the **Accept All** button at the top of the AI panel to confirm every suggestion at once when you trust the AI's plan.
</Tip>

## The Right Panel

Click the **panel toggle button** (⊢) to open or close the side panel. The panel has three tabs:

### AI Tab
Shows every pending AI suggestion grouped by technician. Review and accept or reject each one individually, or use the bulk buttons at the top.

### Queue Tab
Shows jobs that haven't been assigned yet and are waiting to be sent to the optimizer. Check the boxes next to jobs you want to dispatch, then click **Dispatch Selected** to send them for optimization. If no jobs are individually checked, the button dispatches all ready jobs at once.

### Visits Tab
Shows all scheduled visits for the current week pulled from your FSM, along with a live status summary: **Scheduled**, **In Progress**, **In Transit**, and **Completed** counts appear at the top. Scroll down to see the full list with technician assignments and addresses.

<Note>
When the right panel is in **Map** view mode, the side panel auto-collapses to give the map more space. Click the toggle button to re-open it if needed.
</Note>

## Unassigned Jobs Bell

In the calendar header you'll see a **bell icon** (🔔). When the optimizer has jobs it couldn't assign — because no technician matched the required skill, the time window was too tight, or no one was available in the area — a small orange badge appears on the bell showing the count.

Click the bell to open the **Unassigned Jobs** panel. Each entry shows:
- **Job number and customer name**
- **Severity level** — Critical (red), Warning (amber), or Info (blue)
- **Why it wasn't assigned** — a plain-language reason like "No technician available with required skills"
- **Possible reasons** — up to two additional contributing factors
- An **Assign Manually** button to open the reassignment dialog directly from the list

At the bottom of the panel, a **Resync All** button re-submits all unassigned jobs to the optimizer for another planning attempt — useful after you've freed up technician availability or added a new team member.

<Tip>
If the bell shows a red badge (Critical), check the Unassigned Jobs panel before ending your dispatch session. Critical jobs are typically same-day with no fallback technician.
</Tip>

## Rejection History

Click the **History** button (clock icon) in the header to view a log of every suggestion you've rejected, including the reasons given. Useful for identifying patterns — for example, if a particular technician's assignments are regularly rejected in a specific area.

## Syncing with Your FSM

The calendar pulls data automatically. To force a refresh — for example, if you've just made changes in your scheduling software — click the **Refresh** button (↺) in the header.

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why is a job still showing as blue after I accepted it?">
    The calendar occasionally needs a moment to refresh. Click the **Refresh** button and the block should turn green.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I drag and drop jobs to reassign them?">
    Not in the current version — reassignments go through the **AI Dispatch** page or the job detail card. Drag-and-drop is on the roadmap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why does a job show a red conflict indicator?">
    A conflict means two jobs overlap in time, or a technician doesn't meet the skill requirements for the job. Click the block to see the conflict details and resolution options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The calendar is empty — where are my jobs?">
    Make sure your scheduling software is connected under **Settings → Connected Apps**, then click **Refresh**. If jobs still don't appear, check **Dashboard → Logs** for sync errors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Timeline Schedule and Timeline Hourly?">
    **Schedule** shows a compact all-day overview — great for quickly scanning who's doing what without worrying about exact times. **Hourly** shows each hour as a separate row so you can see precise start/end times and gaps between jobs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Business Hours mode isn't clipping the grid correctly.">
    Business Hours mode reads from your **Company Business Hours** setting. Go to **Settings** and make sure your working hours are saved correctly, then return to the calendar.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
