# AI Chat

The AI Chat page gives you a conversational interface to your dispatch plan. Ask plain-English questions about your schedule, get constraint explanations, and request optimization changes — without leaving the dashboard.

Navigate to **Dashboard → AI Chat** to open the chat window.

## What You Can Ask

The AI assistant has direct access to your live schedule and optimization engine. Some things you can do:

- **Understand the plan:** "Why was John not assigned any jobs today?"
- **Check conflicts:** "Which jobs have overlapping time windows?"
- **Analyze workload:** "Who has the most driving today?"
- **Request changes:** "Move the 2 PM appointment to someone closer to downtown."
- **Explain scores:** "Why does this assignment have a low confidence score?"

## What Happens While the AI Thinks

While the AI works on your question, you'll see a typing indicator (three animated dots) and small status labels showing exactly what it's doing in real time:

| Status Label | What It Means |
|---|---|
| 🔧 Looking up the dispatch board... | Reading your current schedule |
| 🔧 Finding job details... | Pulling up a specific job's information |
| 🔧 Checking technician availability... | Looking at who's free and when |
| 🔧 Reviewing route summary... | Calculating travel times and routes |
| 🔧 Preparing changes... | Building a plan modification |
| 🔧 Confirming update... | Writing the accepted change back to the plan |
| 🔧 Analyzing constraints... | Examining optimization scores and rule violations |
| 🔧 Checking feasibility... | Testing whether a requested change is possible |
| 🔧 Processing... | General computation in progress |

These status labels disappear once the AI has finished each step and is ready to reply.

## If Something Goes Wrong

The AI Chat will show a friendly message if it can't respond. Here's what each one means and what to do:

| Message | What It Means | What to Do |
|---|---|---|
| "Temporarily unavailable, try again in a few minutes" | The AI service is under heavy load | Wait a moment and send your message again |
| "Undergoing maintenance, try again later" | Scheduled maintenance is in progress | Try again in a little while |
| "Request timed out — try again" | Your question took too long to process | Break it into smaller questions or try again |
| "Too many requests — wait a moment" | You've sent a lot of messages quickly | Pause for 30 seconds and try again |
| "Session expired — please log in again" | Your login session ended | Refresh the page and log in |
| "Not configured for your organization" | AI Chat isn't set up yet for your account | Contact your administrator |
| "Service is offline" | The AI service is unreachable | Contact your administrator |

<Note>
Most errors are temporary. If the same error keeps appearing, contact your account administrator.
</Note>

## How It Works

The AI assistant uses the same optimization engine that powers the AI Dispatch page. When you ask it to make a change, it:

1. Reads the current schedule and constraints
2. Evaluates whether the change is feasible
3. Shows you a preview of the impact
4. Applies the change only after you confirm

<Note>
The AI Chat assistant does **not** automatically write changes to your FSM. Changes made through chat update the AI Dispatcher plan; you still review and accept them on the AI Dispatch page before they sync back.
</Note>

## Tips for Best Results

- Be specific: "Move job #1234 to Sarah" works better than "reassign that job"
- Ask one thing at a time for complex changes
- If the AI says a change isn't feasible, ask it why — it can explain the exact constraint blocking it
- Use the **AI Dispatch** page for bulk approvals; use **AI Chat** for investigation and one-off adjustments
