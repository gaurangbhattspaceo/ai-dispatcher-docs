<Note>
**AI Chat was retired in the April 2026 update.** The `/dashboard/ai-chat` route has been removed from the application. Dispatch managers should use the **AI Dispatch** page for schedule analysis and one-off job changes — the AI Dispatcher optimization engine handles the same reasoning tasks and is the supported path for dispatch decisions going forward.
</Note>

## What Was AI Chat?

AI Chat previously gave dispatch managers a conversational interface to ask plain-English questions about their schedule, request optimization changes, and investigate specific job or technician assignments.

## What to Use Instead

| AI Chat Task | Replacement |
|---|---|
| Understand why a job is unassigned | Check the **Unassigned Jobs Panel** on the AI Dispatch page |
| See who is working today | Filter the **Technicians** page by today's date |
| Request a job reassignment | Use the **Reassignment Dialog** on the AI Dispatch page |
| Explain why a suggestion scored low | Check the **Confidence Score** and **AI Reasoning** panel on each suggestion card |
| One-off schedule changes | Click **Generate Schedule** on AI Dispatch, or use the **Reassignment Dialog** for specific changes |

See the [AI Dispatch documentation](/dashboard/ai-dispatch) for the full workflow.
