# ServiceTrade Integration

Connect your ServiceTrade account to optimize technician dispatch.

## Setup

1. Go to **Settings > Connected Apps**
2. Click **Connect ServiceTrade**
3. Enter your ServiceTrade credentials
4. Data sync begins automatically

## Synced Data

- Service appointments
- Location details
- Technician information
- Equipment assignments

## Disconnecting

Go to **Settings > Connected Apps** and click **Disconnect** next to ServiceTrade.
