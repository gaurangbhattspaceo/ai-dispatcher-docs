# FieldCamp Integration

Connect your FieldCamp account to automatically sync jobs, technicians, and dispatch assignments.

## Setup

1. Go to **Settings > Connected Apps**
2. Click **Connect FieldCamp**
3. Enter your FieldCamp API token
4. Select which data to sync (jobs, technicians, or both)

## How It Works

- **Jobs** are pulled from FieldCamp via webhook or periodic sync
- **Technicians** and their profiles are imported automatically
- **Dispatch assignments** are pushed back to FieldCamp after approval

## Webhook Configuration

For real-time job syncing, configure a webhook in your FieldCamp settings pointing to your AI Dispatcher webhook endpoint. See the [Webhooks API](/api/webhooks) for details.
