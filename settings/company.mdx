This is where you tell AI Dispatcher when your company works. The AI uses these hours as the default scheduling window for all jobs — it won't schedule a technician outside these hours unless they have their own individual schedule set up.

## Setting Your Business Hours

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    In the left sidebar, click **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Company Business Hours">
    The Business Hours card is at the top of the Settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable your working days">
    Check the box next to each day your team works. Unchecked days are treated as non-working days — no jobs will be scheduled on those days by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set start and end times">
    For each checked day, enter your **start time** (when technicians begin work) and **end time** (when their day ends). The default is 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM.

    For example: Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Save Business Hours">
    Your hours are saved immediately. The next planning run will use these hours to schedule jobs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Set your hours **before** running AI dispatch for the first time. If you skip this step, the AI uses a default 9–5 schedule which may not match your actual workday.
</Tip>

## How Business Hours Affect Dispatch

When the AI plans your day, it uses your business hours to determine when jobs can be scheduled:

- Jobs won't be scheduled to start before your **start time**
- Jobs won't be scheduled to run past your **end time**
- Days with unchecked boxes are skipped entirely when scheduling multi-day job queues

**Individual technician schedules take priority.** If a specific technician has their own availability set (on the Technicians page), those hours override the company defaults for that person.

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if some technicians work different hours?">
    Set the company hours to your most common schedule. Then, on the **Technicians** page, you can set individual availability for anyone whose hours differ from the default.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="We have emergency or on-call service outside business hours — how do I handle that?">
    Business hours define the default planning window. For emergency jobs, you can manually assign a technician from the **AI Dispatch** page regardless of their scheduled hours.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to add holidays separately?">
    Currently, you can mark specific days as non-working by unchecking them temporarily. If your holiday schedule changes week to week, update the hours before running dispatch for that week.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
