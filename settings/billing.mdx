## Subscription Plans

Your current plan determines how many jobs and technicians you can manage, and how many AI dispatch runs you can do each month. Go to **Settings → Billing** to view your current plan and upgrade or downgrade as your needs change.

## Credits

AI dispatch optimization runs on a **credit system**. Each time you run the AI optimizer, it uses a small number of credits based on the size of the job queue.

From the Billing page you can:
- See your **current credit balance**
- Review your **usage history** (how many credits each run used)
- **Purchase additional credits** if you're running low

<Tip>
Credits never expire. If you buy extra credits and don't use them this month, they roll over automatically.
</Tip>

## Updating Payment Details

Click **Manage Billing** (or **Open Payment Portal**) to open the Stripe customer portal. From there you can:
- Update your credit card
- View and download past invoices
- Change your billing email

<Note>
Payment processing is handled by **Stripe**, a secure payment platform. AI Dispatcher never stores your card details directly.
</Note>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many credits does one AI dispatch run use?">
    Credit usage depends on the number of jobs and technicians in the run. A typical run with 10–20 jobs uses 1–3 credits. You can see the exact usage for each run in your credit history.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
    The AI optimizer will be paused until you purchase more credits. Manual dispatch (assigning technicians yourself on the Calendar or Jobs page) still works without credits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    Open the Stripe payment portal from the Billing page and select **Cancel plan**. Your account remains active through the end of the current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
