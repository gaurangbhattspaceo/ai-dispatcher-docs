The **Settings** area is where you configure how AI Dispatcher works for your team. You can reach it by clicking **Settings** in the left sidebar from any page.

## What's in Settings

Settings is organized into two main sections:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Company Business Hours" icon="clock">
    Set the default working days and hours for your team. The AI won't schedule jobs outside these hours.
    [Go to Company Settings →](/settings/company)
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Optimization Settings" icon="sliders">
    Choose an optimization preset or fine-tune the four sliders that control how the AI balances job assignment, time windows, travel, and workload.
    [Go to AI Settings →](/settings/ai-dispatcher)
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Quick Links

| Setting | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Business Hours** | Sets when the AI can schedule jobs for your team |
| **Optimization Preset** | One-click configuration: Balanced, Minimize Travel, Fill All Jobs, or Balance Workload |
| **Optimization Sliders** | Fine-grained control over the four AI priorities |
| **Preferred Technician** | How strongly the AI favors sending the same tech to repeat customers |

## Connected Apps

To connect or manage your scheduling platform integration (FieldCamp, Jobber, HouseCall Pro, ServiceTrade), go to [**Settings → Connected Apps**](/settings/connected-apps).
