## How to Connect a Platform

Each integration has its own setup guide — click the links above for step-by-step instructions. The general flow is:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Connected Apps">
    Find the platform you use and click **Connect [Platform Name]**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize access">
    Depending on the platform: enter your API token, complete the OAuth flow, or enter your credentials. AI Dispatcher guides you through each step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data syncs automatically">
    Once connected, your jobs and technicians appear in AI Dispatcher immediately. From this point on, new jobs sync in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sync Behavior

| Data | Direction | Timing |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Jobs (new and updated) | Your platform → AI Dispatcher | Real-time via webhook |
| Technician list | Your platform → AI Dispatcher | On connect + manual sync |
| Accepted dispatch assignments | AI Dispatcher → Your platform | When you click Accept |

<Note>
Only **one integration** can be active per organization at a time. To switch platforms, disconnect the current one first.
</Note>

## Disconnecting a Platform

To remove a connection:

1. Go to **Settings → Connected Apps**
2. Find the active integration
3. Click **Disconnect**

Disconnecting stops all syncing and removes your credentials from AI Dispatcher. It does not change anything in your scheduling platform.

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My jobs aren't syncing after connecting">
    Go to the **Jobs** page and click **Sync from [Platform]** to trigger a manual import. If the problem continues, try disconnecting and reconnecting to refresh the credentials.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect to more than one platform?">
    Not at the same time. AI Dispatcher supports one active integration per organization. Disconnect your current platform before connecting a new one.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are my credentials stored securely?">
    Yes. OAuth tokens are stored encrypted. API tokens and credentials (for FieldCamp and ServiceTrade) are also encrypted at rest and never displayed after saving.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
